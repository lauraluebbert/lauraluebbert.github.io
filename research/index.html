Research

Viral RNA Detection in Single-Cell RNA Sequencing

June 2022 – January 2025 | Caltech Pachter Lab

Virology scRNA-seq kallisto C++

Expanded the kallisto tool to enable translated alignment of nucleotide sequences against amino acid references while preserving single-cell resolution. This technique identifies viral RNA in sequencing datasets using conserved protein motifs.

This is the first software enabling translated alignment at single-cell resolution, characterizing viral tropism and host responses through parallel analysis of viral presence and gene expression.

Nature Biotechnology (2025) Nature Protocols (2025)

gget - Genomic Database Query Tool

October 2021 – Ongoing | Caltech Pachter Lab

Open Source Python Genomics CLI Tool

Developed an open-source command-line tool and Python package enabling efficient programmatic access to genomic reference databases. The tool has exceeded 400,000 downloads since its May 2022 release and has >1,100 GitHub stars.

CAR T Cell Therapy Clinical Trials

Immunotherapy Cancer Clinical Trial

Analyzed transcriptomic datasets from 12 prostate cancer patients receiving CAR T cell therapy for solid tumors. Examined single-cell gene expression and immune repertoire data from blood, CAR T products, and tumor tissue at multiple timepoints.

Zebra Finch Brain Circuit Recovery

September 2020 – December 2023 | Caltech Pachter Lab

Neuroscience scRNA-seq Songbirds

Analyzed the first single-cell RNA sequencing dataset from zebra finch HVC brain regions before and after chronic interneuron silencing via tetanus toxin. Results demonstrated that the adult brain can overcome extended periods of abnormal activity and precisely restore complex behavior.

SSRI Biosensor Development

April 2018 – December 2018 | Caltech Lester Lab

Biosensors Pharmacology GFP

Created a genetically engineered biosensor using periplasmic binding proteins coupled to circularly permuted GFP to track selective serotonin reuptake inhibitor drug action within cellular compartments, particularly the endoplasmic reticulum.

Organoids Microfluidics Brain Models
